# Language Demand Research - Bahasa Indonesia

## Summary
- Indonesia shows strong AI adoption momentum with high year on year growth in business adoption.
- English proficiency is in the low proficiency band, which increases the value of localized learning materials.
- Indonesia has the largest digital economy GMV in Southeast Asia, supporting demand for practical AI app skills.

## Evidence
- AWS research reports a 47 percent year on year growth rate of AI adoption and that 28 percent of businesses have already adopted AI in Indonesia.
- EF English Proficiency Index lists Indonesia in the low proficiency band with a score of 471 and rank 80 globally.
- e-Conomy SEA 2024 states Indonesia digital economy GMV is expected to reach $90B in 2024, the largest in Southeast Asia.
