# Style Guide - Bahasa Indonesia

## Voice
- Clear, practical, and learner friendly.
- Use short sentences and direct instructions.
- Avoid slang, jokes, or region specific idioms.

## Grammar and punctuation
- Use standard Indonesian grammar and spelling.
- Keep headings in Title Case where reasonable.
- Keep lists short and consistent.

## Formatting rules
- Preserve Markdown structure and headings exactly.
- Keep the required section order.
- Keep tables, checkboxes, and callouts in the same places.

## Consistency
- Use glossary terms consistently.
- Keep product names and technical terms unchanged.

## Cultural adaptation
- Use examples relevant to Indonesia and global remote work.
- Avoid references to US only legal or tax specifics.
