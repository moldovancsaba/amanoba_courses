# Target Language Decision

## Decision
- Target language: Bahasa Indonesia

## Reasoning
- Strong AI adoption momentum in Indonesia with large business uptake.
- Low English proficiency increases the value of localized instruction.
- Large and growing digital economy supports demand for practical AI app skills.

## Sources
- AWS Research on AI adoption in Indonesia, 2025-08-07.
- EF English Proficiency Index, Indonesia profile, 2025.
- e-Conomy SEA 2024, Google Indonesia blog, 2024-11-13.
