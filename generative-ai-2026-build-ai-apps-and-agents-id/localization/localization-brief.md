# Localization Brief - Bahasa Indonesia

## Audience
- Digital nomads and independent builders in Indonesia who already use AI daily.
- Early stage founders and solo builders aiming to ship a commercial AI app.

## Tone
- Clear, direct, practical.
- Supportive and professional.
- Avoid slang and heavy idioms.

## Cultural adaptation goals
- Use examples relevant to remote work and small businesses in Southeast Asia.
- Avoid US-only legal or tax specifics.
- Use common local terms for business and payments where appropriate.

## Content scope
- Full lesson and quiz localization.
- Keep product names and technical terms in English when standard.
