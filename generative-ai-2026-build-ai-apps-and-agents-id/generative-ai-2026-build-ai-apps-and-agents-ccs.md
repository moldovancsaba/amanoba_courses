## Purpose
Teach digital nomads to build and launch a sellable AI app with agents, payments, and a clear path to iteration.

## Key sections (in JSON)
- **Intent**: outcomes and explicit non-goals.
- **Quality gates**: 20 to 30 min lessons; quizzes have at least 7 questions, at least 5 application, at least 2 critical, and 0 recall.
- **Concepts**: product promise, validation, prompts, tools, workflows, deployment, payments, and growth basics.
- **Procedures**:
  - Define problem, user, and promise, then scope MVP
  - Build and test AI features with prompts, tools, and guardrails
  - Deploy, monetize, and iterate using feedback and metrics
- **Assessment blueprint**:
  - Day 15: MVP scope, agent workflow, and cost guard checklist
  - Day 30: Commercial AI app launch kit and 30 day iteration roadmap
- **Lessons**: 30 day program (see `lessons[]` in canonical JSON)
