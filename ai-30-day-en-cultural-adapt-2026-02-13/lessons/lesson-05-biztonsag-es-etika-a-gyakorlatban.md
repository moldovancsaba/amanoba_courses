**Time:** 20-30 minutes **Tangible Output:** AI Security Checklist

Processing of complaints involving personal data. In this situation, you are an HR administrator and you have to make a quick but reliable decision.
By the end of today's practical task, you will have an AI safety checklist.
Key insight: data security and ethics start to work when you make the output measurable right from the start.

At the end of the lesson, you will be able to: **filter and anonymize sensitive data**

## What

### What is this?

This lesson shows in practice how to apply the **data security and ethics** method in a real work situation.
The goal is not a theory, but an output that you can really use in your next working day.

### What isn't this?

This is not a generic AI demo, and it is not busywork.

### 2-minute theory

- Speed cannot override safety. Inspection is a mandatory part of the process.
- The key is that you always translate the task into a clear output.
- Quality comes from ensuring that the AI ​​safety checklist is verifiable and repeatable.

### Key concepts

- **Working framework:** a short guide that describes the three objectives, format and limits.
- **Quality gate:** quick check before accepting the output.

## Where

### It works fine here

- processing a complaint case containing personal data in situations where a quick and clear decision is required
- when an AI safety checklist gives multiple people a clear next step

### Do not use this when

- if you cannot cover the risk of legal and reputational damage with human control

### Connection points

- Daily handovers between HR administrator and legal advisor
- short statuses and decision preparation notes
- repetitive tasks, where 3 out of 3 sample materials are correctly anonymized

## When

### Use it when

- you are faced with the task of processing a complaint case containing personal data, and you have little time
- Based on the AI safety checklist, several actors must act in the same way

### Frequency

At least once per workday, and every time for recurring tasks.

### Late warning signs

- AI safety checklist needs to be rewritten every time
- you often get back that the content does not address legal and reputational damage issues

## Why it matters

### Practical benefits

- AI security checklist is created faster, with fewer rounds
- the quality will be more measurable with 3 error-free anonymized indicators from 3 sample materials
- rework is reduced, because you filter out legal and reputational damage errors early

### What happens if you skip it

- the number of misunderstandings increases when processing a complaint handling case containing personal data
- it is more difficult to maintain 3 error-free anonymized targets from 3 sample materials

### Realistic expectation
- It will improve: the practical application of data security and ethics and the quality of the transferable output.
- Not guaranteed: infallible response without human control in every run.

## How

### Step by step

1. Name the work situation and the expected result exactly. 2. Write down the mandatory limits (time frame, scope, style, prohibitions). 3. Request the first AI version in a specific format. 4. Run a 3-point quality gate, paying special attention to **legal and reputational damage** risk. 5. Fix the output, then save it as a usable template.

### Do / Don't

**Do**
- Provide a short, specific task description so that the AI security checklist format is clear from the start.
- Before finalizing, check whether 3 anonymized measurement points from 3 sample materials have been completed without errors.


**Don't**
- Don't leave the topic of legal and reputational damage in the background, even if the answer seems good at first.
- Do not ask for a general text if you need material for a specific decision for the task of processing a complaint handling case containing personal data.

### Common errors and their correction

- **Error:** too general a request for the processing of a complaint handling case containing personal data. **Correction:** name the person concerned, the deadline and the decision target.
- **Error:** there is no connection between the output and 3 error-free anonymized measurements from 3 sample materials. **Fix:** record 1 binary pass/fail result at the end of each run.
