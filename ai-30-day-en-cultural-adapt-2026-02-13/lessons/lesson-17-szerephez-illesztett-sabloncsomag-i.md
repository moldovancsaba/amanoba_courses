Unify sales and customer support responses. In this situation, you are an inside salesperson and you have to make a quick but reliable decision.
By the end of today's practical task, you will have 6 message templates ready for situations, which you can use again tomorrow.

At the end of the lesson, you will be able to: **standardize recurring communication situations**

## What

### What is this?

This lesson shows in practice how to apply the **communication template package** method in a real work situation.
The goal is not a theory, but an output that you can really use in your next working day.

### What isn't this?

This is not a generic AI demo, and it is not busywork.

### 2-minute theory

- The template is not rigidity, but quick quality assurance.
- The key is that you always translate the task into a clear output.
- Quality comes from ensuring that 6 message templates ready for situations is verifiable and repeatable.

### Key concepts

- **Working framework:** a short guide that describes the three objectives, format and limits.
- **Quality gate:** quick check before accepting the output.

## Where

### It works fine here

- unification of sales and customer support responses in situations where a quick and clear decision is required
- when 6 situation-ready message templates give clear next steps to several people

### Do not use this when

- if you cannot cover the risk of disintegrating style and quality with human control

### Connection points

- daily handovers between internal sales and customer support staff
- short statuses and decision preparation notes
- repetitive tasks, where all 6 templates count in usable test situations

## When

### Use it when

- you are faced with the task of standardizing sales and customer support responses, and time is short
- Based on 6 situation-ready message templates, several actors must act in the same way

### Frequency

At least once per workday, and every time for recurring tasks.

### Late warning signs

- 6 message templates ready for situations need to be rewritten every time
- you often get back that the content does not deal with the disintegrating style and quality problem

## Why it matters

### Practical benefits

- 6 message templates ready for situations are created faster, with fewer rounds
- the quality will be more measurable, each of the 6 templates can be used in a test situation with an indicator
- post-work is reduced, because you filter out disintegrating style and quality errors early

### What happens if you skip it

- the number of misunderstandings increases in the situation of unifying sales and customer support responses
- it is more difficult to keep 6 templates, each of which can be used in a test situation

### Realistic expectations

- It will improve: the practical application of the communication template package and the quality of the deliverable output.
- Not guaranteed: infallible response without human control in every run.

## How

### Step by step

1. Name the work situation and the expected result exactly. 2. Write down the mandatory limits (time frame, scope, style, prohibitions). 3. Request the first AI version in a specific format. 4. Run a 3-point quality gate, paying particular attention to the risk of **disintegrating style and quality**. 5. Fix the output, then save it as a usable template.

### Do / Don't

**Do**
- Enter a short, specific task description so that the format of the 6 situation-ready message templates is clear from the start.
- Before finalizing, check whether all 6 templates can be used in a test situation.


**Don't**
- Don't let the topic of style and quality fall apart in the background, even if the answer seems good at first.
- Do not ask for a general text if you need a specific decision for the task of unifying material sales and customer support responses.

### Common errors and their correction

- **Error:** Too general a request to unify sales and customer support responses to a situation. **Correction:** name the person concerned, the deadline and the decision target.
- **Error:** there is no connection between the output and all 6 templates can be used for measurement in a test situation. **Fix:** record 1 binary pass/fail result at the end of each run.
