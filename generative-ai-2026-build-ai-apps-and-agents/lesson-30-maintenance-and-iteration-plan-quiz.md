**Question:** Why limit the roadmap to three priorities?

A) To ignore feedback for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) To keep focus and avoid overload
C) To slow progress when validating a paid AI workflow
D) To remove accountability for a first release with limited time

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** Fewer priorities keep work focused.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Not the goal.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Not true.
- D: Not true.
