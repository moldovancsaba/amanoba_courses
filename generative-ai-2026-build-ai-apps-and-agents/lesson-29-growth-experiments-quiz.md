**Question:** Why run only one growth experiment at a time?

A) To reduce learning for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) To keep focus and learn clearly from results
C) To avoid tracking metrics
D) To increase costs for a first release with limited time

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** One experiment keeps learning clean.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Not a goal.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Not true.
- D: Not a benefit.
