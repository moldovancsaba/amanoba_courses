**Question:** What is the best starting pricing layout for a new product?

A) Ten complex tiers for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) One clear plan with a single price
C) Hidden pricing when validating a paid AI workflow
D) A custom quote only for a first release with limited time

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** One plan reduces decision friction.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Too complex.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Frustrating.
- D: Not ideal early.
