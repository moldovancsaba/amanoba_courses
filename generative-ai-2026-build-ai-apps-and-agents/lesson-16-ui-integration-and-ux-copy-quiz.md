**Question:** Why are loading states important? in a small commercial AI app scenario

A) They make the UI slower for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) They show the system is working and reduce confusion
C) They replace error handling
D) They increase costs for a first release with limited time

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** Users need feedback that the system is working.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Not true.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Not a replacement.
- D: Not related.
