# Lesson 4 Quiz Pool

**Question:** Which value proposition is the strongest?

A) "AI tools for everyone" for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) "Freelance designers generate a client proposal draft in under 10 minutes"
C) "A platform with templates, AI, and analytics"
D) "The best AI app on the market"

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** It names a user and a clear outcome.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Too broad.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Feature list.
- D: Not specific.

**Tags:** #value-prop #difficulty-easy #type-scenario

**Question:** A pricing hypothesis must include which three elements?

A) A number, a unit, and a target buyer
B) A logo, a name, and a tagline
C) A long roadmap and a feature list
D) A press release and a demo video

**Correct:** A

**Why correct:** Pricing is testable only with a number, unit, and buyer.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: This is correct.
- B: Branding elements are not pricing.
- C: Not pricing.
- D: Not pricing.
