**Question:** Why include a go or no go decision? in a small commercial AI app scenario

A) To add more meetings for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) To prevent rushed launches when critical issues exist
C) To delay forever when validating a paid AI workflow
D) To increase costs for a first release with limited time

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** A final decision step prevents launching with blockers.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Not the goal.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Not a goal.
- D: Not a benefit.
