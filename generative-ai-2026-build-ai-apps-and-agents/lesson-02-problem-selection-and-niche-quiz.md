# Lesson 2 Quiz Pool

**Question:** A builder is choosing between three ideas. Which evidence is the strongest buyer signal?

A) A Twitter poll with 200 votes for the idea
B) A paid tool with customers in the same niche and rising prices
C) A Reddit thread with many upvotes
D) Friends say the idea sounds cool

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** Paying customers and rising prices show willingness to pay.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Votes show interest, not payment.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Upvotes are not purchases.
- D: Friends are not buyers.

**Tags:** #buyer-signals #difficulty-easy #type-scenario

**Question:** A problem statement says, "People need better AI tools." What is the best fix?

A) Keep it and move on to features
B) Replace it with a specific user, workflow, and outcome
C) Add more adjectives like fast and smart
D) Add more tools to the stack

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** Specificity is required to validate demand and pick a niche.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Vague problems lead to weak validation.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Adjectives are not evidence.
- D: Tools do not define the problem.

**Tags:** #problem-statement #difficulty-easy #type-error
