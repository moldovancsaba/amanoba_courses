**Question:** Why are rate limits important for an AI app?

A) They make the UI faster for a solo builder shipping an MVP
B) They prevent a few users from consuming all budget
C) They replace pricing when validating a paid AI workflow
D) They increase model quality

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** Rate limits protect budget and stability.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Not the main reason.
- B: This is correct.
- C: Not a replacement.
- D: Not related.
