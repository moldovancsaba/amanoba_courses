# Localization Brief

Course: Generative AI 2026: Build AI Apps and Agents
Target language: Swahili
Date: 2026-02-07

## Purpose
Localize the course for Swahili speaking learners with cultural adaptation. Keep the structure and assessment standards identical to the source course.

## Tone and formality
- Clear, professional, and practical.
- Use neutral, standard Swahili that works across East Africa.
- Avoid slang or region specific idioms unless widely understood.

## Technical depth
- Keep the same technical depth as the English source.
- Keep product, programming, and API terms accurate and consistent.

## Cultural adaptation rules
- Adapt examples to local contexts when possible.
- Use neutral currency examples (USD) unless local currency improves clarity.
- Avoid references that require US only services beyond OpenAI, GitHub, Vercel, Stripe.
- Replace region specific assumptions with global equivalents.

## Do not translate
- Product and company names: OpenAI, GitHub, Vercel, Stripe, ChatGPT.
- API, endpoint, webhook, and code keywords.

## Localization outputs
- Full lesson content, quizzes, and emails in Swahili.
- All required sections and quiz structure must be preserved.
