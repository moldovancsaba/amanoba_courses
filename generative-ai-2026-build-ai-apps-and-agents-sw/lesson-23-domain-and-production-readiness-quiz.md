# Lesson 23 Quiz Pool

**Question:** Kwa nini SSL ni muhimu kwa app ya production?

A) Inaongeza muda wa response kwa makusudi.
B) Inalinda data inaposafiri kwenye mtandao.
C) Inaondoa hitaji la domain.
D) Inaongeza gharama bila faida.

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** SSL hulinda data dhidi ya kuingiliwa.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Latency si lengo.
- C: Domain bado inahitajika.
- D: SSL ina faida kubwa.

**Tags:** #security #difficulty-easy #type-definition

**Question:** Ni hatua ipi bora kabla ya uzinduzi katika mazingira ya app ya AI ya kibiashara.?

A) Ku-launch bila health check.
B) Kuthibitisha health check na SSL.
C) Kuacha DNS records bila uhakiki.
D) Kuacha production bila domain.

**Correct:** B

**Why correct:** Health check na SSL hutoa uthibitisho wa utayari.
**Why others are wrong:**
- A: Hii ni hatari.
- C: DNS isiyo sahihi huleta down.
- D: Domain husaidia uaminifu.
