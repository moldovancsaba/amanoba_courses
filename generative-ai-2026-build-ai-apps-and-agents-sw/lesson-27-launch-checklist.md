# Lesson 27: Launch checklist

**One-liner:** Hakikisha uzinduzi unaenda vizuri kwa checklist ya hatua za mwisho.  
**Time:** 20 hadi 30 dakika  
**Deliverable:** Launch Checklist

## Learning goal

You will be able to: **Kuandaa checklist ya uzinduzi na kuifanya iwe rahisi kufuata.**

### Success criteria (observable)
- [ ] Checklist ina angalau vipengele 10.
- [ ] Kipengele 1 kina owner.
- [ ] Kipengele 1 kina status ya kupita au kushindwa.

### Output you will produce
- **Deliverable:** Launch Checklist
- **Format:** Orodha ya ukaguzi
- **Where saved:** Kwenye folda ya kozi ndani ya `/generative-ai-2026-build-ai-apps-and-agents-sw/`

## Who

**Primary persona:** Digital nomad anayeandaa uzinduzi wa app
**Secondary persona(s):** Watumiaji wa kwanza
**Stakeholders (optional):** Washirika wa ujenzi

## What

### What it is
Orodha ya hatua za mwisho kabla ya uzinduzi.
Inasaidia kuhakikisha hakuna kitu muhimu kinachosahaulika.

### What it is not
Si roadmap ya miezi 6.
Si mpango wa masoko wa kina.

### 2-minute theory
- Checklist hupunguza makosa ya dakika ya mwisho.
- Kuwa na owner huongeza uwajibikaji.
- Status husaidia kuona kilichokamilika.

### Key terms
- **Launch checklist:** Orodha ya hatua za mwisho kabla ya uzinduzi.
- **Owner:** Mtu anayewajibika kwa kipengele.

## Where

### Applies in
- Release management
- Team coordination

### Does not apply in
- Demos zisizo na launch

### Touchpoints
- Checklist doc
- Release notes
- Status board

## When

### Use it when
- Uzinduzi uko karibu
- Unahitaji kufuatilia kazi za mwisho

### Frequency
Kila uzinduzi

### Late signals
- Hatua muhimu zinaachwa nyuma
- Hakuna uwajibikaji wa kazi

## Why it matters

### Practical benefits
- Uzinduzi laini
- Uwazi wa hali ya kazi
- Makosa machache baada ya launch

### Risks of ignoring
- Uzinduzi wenye makosa
- Kazi muhimu kusahaulika

### Expectations
- Improves: uratibu na ubora wa uzinduzi
- Does not guarantee: uzinduzi bila matatizo

## How

### Step-by-step method
1. Orodhesha hatua 10 muhimu.
2. Weka owner kwa kila hatua.
3. Weka status ya pass au fail.
4. Pitia checklist siku moja kabla.
5. Rekebisha kipengele chochote kilicho fail.

### Do and don't

**Do**
- Weka hatua fupi na zinazopimika
- Sasisha status mara kwa mara

**Don't**
- Kuacha checklist bila owner
- Kuongeza vitu visivyo na maana

### Common mistakes and fixes
- **Mistake:** Hatua ni pana sana. **Fix:** Zigawanye.
- **Mistake:** Hakuna status. **Fix:** Ongeza pass au fail.

### Done when
- [ ] Checklist ina hatua 10.
- [ ] Owner ameteuliwa.
- [ ] Status zimesasishwa.
