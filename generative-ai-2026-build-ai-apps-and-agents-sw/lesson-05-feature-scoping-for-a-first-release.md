# Lesson 5: Kuweka wigo wa toleo la kwanza

**One-liner:** Fafanua seti ndogo ya vipengele vinavyoweza kuuzwa.  
**Time:** 20 hadi 30 dakika  
**Deliverable:** Orodha ya Wigo wa MVP

## Learning goal

You will be able to: **Kuunda wigo wa MVP unaoendana na ahadi ya bidhaa.**

### Success criteria (observable)
- [ ] Orodha ina must have na later.
- [ ] Kila must have inaendana na matokeo ya ahadi.
- [ ] Wigo unatosha kwa toleo la kwanza.

### Output you will produce
- **Deliverable:** Orodha ya Wigo wa MVP
- **Format:** Orodha ya safu mbili
- **Where saved:** Kwenye folda ya kozi ndani ya `/generative-ai-2026-build-ai-apps-and-agents-sw/`

## Who

**Primary persona:** Digital nomad anayefafanua wigo wa MVP
**Secondary persona(s):** Watumiaji na wanunuzi wa awali
**Stakeholders (optional):** Washirika

## What

### What it is
Orodha fupi ya must have inayothibitisha ahadi ya bidhaa kwa toleo la kwanza.
Inalenga matokeo ambayo watumiaji watalipia.

### What it is not
Si roadmap kamili wala bidhaa ya baadaye.
Si orodha kubwa ya vipengele vyote.

### 2-minute theory
- Wigo unapaswa kuthibitisha ahadi haraka.
- Vipengele vya baadaye vinaweza kusubiri.
- MVP ndogo hupunguza muda na gharama ya kujifunza.

### Key terms
- **MVP:** Minimum viable product inayoweza kulipiwa.
- **Must have:** Vipengele vinavyohitajika kufikia matokeo.

## Where

### Applies in
- Mpango wa bidhaa
- Upangaji wa kazi

### Does not apply in
- Uboreshaji wa miundombinu

### Touchpoints
- Backlog
- Roadmap
- Sprint planning

## When

### Use it when
- Ahadi na JTBD ziko wazi
- Unahitaji kuchagua nini cha kujenga kwanza

### Frequency
Mara moja kwa kila wazo, kisha rekebisha baada ya feedback

### Late signals
- Unaongeza vipengele kabla ya launch
- MVP ni kubwa kupita mwezi mmoja

## Why it matters

### Practical benefits
- Launch ya haraka
- Vipaumbele wazi
- Uthibitishaji rahisi

### Risks of ignoring
- Kuchelewa kuwasilisha
- Feature creep

### Expectations
- Improves: umakini na kasi
- Does not guarantee: traction

## How

### Step-by-step method
1. Orodhesha vipengele vyote vinavyowezekana.
2. Chagua vinavyohitajika kuthibitisha ahadi.
3. Hamisha vilivyobaki kwenda later.
4. Hakikisha must have ni ndogo na ya kuezeka.

### Do and don't

**Do**
- Funga must have na matokeo ya mtumiaji
- Weka orodha fupi

**Don't**
- Ongeza vipengele kwa kila hali
- Changanya nice to have na must have

### Common mistakes and fixes
- **Mistake:** MVP kubwa sana. **Fix:** Ondoa vipengele visivyo lazima.
- **Mistake:** Hatua muhimu inakosekana. **Fix:** Ongeza hatua muhimu tu.

### Done when
- [ ] Must have ina vipengele 3 hadi 6.
- [ ] Kila kipengele kinaendana na ahadi.
- [ ] Later ni ndefu kuliko must have.

## Guided exercise (10 to 15 min)

### Inputs
- Ahadi ya bidhaa
- Orodha ya vipengele vya uwezekano

### Steps
1. Angazia vipengele vinavyothibitisha ahadi.
2. Hamisha vingine kwenda later.
3. Punguza hadi idadi ndogo.

### Output format
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Must have | |
| Later | |
| Ulinganifu wa ahadi | |
| Hatari ya launch | |

## Independent exercise (5 to 10 min)

### Task
Ondoa kipengele kimoja kutoka must have na eleza sababu.

### Output
Orodha iliyosasishwa ya MVP na sababu fupi.
