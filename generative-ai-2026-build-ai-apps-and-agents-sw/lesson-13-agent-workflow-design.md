# Lesson 13: Muundo wa workflow ya agent

**One-liner:** Panga hatua za agent ili ziwe wazi, ndogo, na zinazoweza kupimwa.  
**Time:** 20 hadi 30 dakika  
**Deliverable:** Ramani ya Workflow ya Agent na Hatua

## Learning goal

You will be able to: **Kubuni workflow ya agent yenye hatua wazi na vigezo vya mafanikio.**

### Success criteria (observable)
- [ ] Workflow ina hatua angalau 4 zinazofuatana.
- [ ] Kila hatua ina input na output iliyoelezwa.
- [ ] Hatua moja ina guardrail au verification.

### Output you will produce
- **Deliverable:** Ramani ya Workflow ya Agent na Hatua
- **Format:** Orodha ya hatua na mchoro rahisi
- **Where saved:** Kwenye folda ya kozi ndani ya `/generative-ai-2026-build-ai-apps-and-agents-sw/`

## Who

**Primary persona:** Digital nomad anayebuni workflow ya agent
**Secondary persona(s):** Watumiaji wanaotegemea matokeo sahihi
**Stakeholders (optional):** Washirika wa ujenzi

## What

### What it is
Mpangilio wa hatua ndogo ambazo agent hufuata ili kutoa matokeo.
Inaleta uwazi kwa input, output, na hatua za uamuzi.

### What it is not
Si mtiririko mrefu wa hatua zisizo na mipaka.
Si mbadala wa logic ya bidhaa au approvals.

### 2-minute theory
- Workflow iliyo wazi hupunguza makosa na kurudia kazi.
- Hatua ndogo hufanya agent kuwa rahisi kupima.
- Guardrails huzuia matokeo ya hatari.

### Key terms
- **Workflow:** Mpangilio wa hatua zinazofuatana kufikia lengo.
- **Guardrail:** Sheria inayozuia hatua hatari.

## Where

### Applies in
- Agent orchestration
- Backend flows

### Does not apply in
- Tasks moja inayokamilika kwa prompt moja

### Touchpoints
- Flow diagrams
- Step logs
- Test scenarios

## When

### Use it when
- Agent ina hatua nyingi
- Unahitaji ufuatiliaji wa makosa

### Frequency
Kila unapoanzisha agent mpya

### Late signals
- Agent kuruka hatua muhimu
- Output kutokueleweka

## Why it matters

### Practical benefits
- Uthabiti wa matokeo
- Debugging ya haraka
- Usalama wa actions

### Risks of ignoring
- Workflow isiyoeleweka
- Matokeo yasiyotabirika

### Expectations
- Improves: uelewa na uthabiti
- Does not guarantee: matokeo kamili

## How

### Step-by-step method
1. Tambua lengo la mwisho la agent.
2. Gawa kazi kuwa hatua ndogo 4 hadi 6.
3. Weka input na output kwa kila hatua.
4. Ongeza hatua ya verification au guardrail.
5. Andika mkondo wa data kati ya hatua.

### Do and don't

**Do**
- Weka hatua fupi na zinazoeleweka
- Log hatua muhimu

**Don't**
- Kuruka hatua za uthibitisho
- Kuunganisha tasks nyingi kwenye hatua moja

### Common mistakes and fixes
- **Mistake:** Hatua kubwa mno. **Fix:** Gawanya kuwa hatua mbili.
- **Mistake:** Hakuna verification. **Fix:** Ongeza check ya ubora.

### Done when
- [ ] Hatua zote zina input na output.
- [ ] Kuna angalau guardrail moja.
- [ ] Workflow ina mchoro au orodha iliyo wazi.
