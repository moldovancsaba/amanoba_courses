# Lesson 29: Growth experiments

**One-liner:** Fanya majaribio madogo ya ukuaji na pima matokeo.  
**Time:** 20 hadi 30 dakika  
**Deliverable:** Mpango wa Growth Experiments

## Learning goal

You will be able to: **Kubuni majaribio 2 ya ukuaji na kipimo cha matokeo.**

### Success criteria (observable)
- [ ] Majaribio 2 yameandikwa.
- [ ] Kila jaribio lina hypothesis na metric.
- [ ] Timeline ya wiki 1 imeandikwa.

### Output you will produce
- **Deliverable:** Mpango wa Growth Experiments
- **Format:** Orodha ya majaribio na metrics
- **Where saved:** Kwenye folda ya kozi ndani ya `/generative-ai-2026-build-ai-apps-and-agents-sw/`

## Who

**Primary persona:** Digital nomad anayefanya majaribio ya ukuaji
**Secondary persona(s):** Watumiaji wapya
**Stakeholders (optional):** Washirika wa ujenzi

## What

### What it is
Majaribio madogo yenye hypothesis na metric ya kupima.
Inasaidia kujifunza kwa data badala ya hisia.

### What it is not
Si mpango mkubwa wa masoko.
Si kampeni ya gharama kubwa.

### 2-minute theory
- Majaribio madogo hupunguza gharama na hatari.
- Hypothesis husaidia kujua unachopima.
- Metric moja nzuri ni bora kuliko kumi zisizo wazi.

### Key terms
- **Experiment:** Jaribio lenye hypothesis na metric.
- **Conversion:** Asilimia ya watumiaji wanaofanya hatua muhimu.

## Where

### Applies in
- Landing page tests
- Channel tests

### Does not apply in
- Product stability work

### Touchpoints
- Signup funnel
- Marketing copy
- Analytics dashboard

## When

### Use it when
- Unataka kuongeza watumiaji
- Una hypothesis ya ukuaji

### Frequency
Majaribio 1 hadi 2 kwa wiki

### Late signals
- Ukuaji unasimama
- Hakuna data ya kuamua

## Why it matters

### Practical benefits
- Uamuzi wa ukuaji unaotokana na data
- Matumizi bora ya muda
- Kuepuka kampeni zisizo na matokeo

### Risks of ignoring
- Muda kupotea kwenye mambo yasiyo na maana
- Kukosa fursa za ukuaji

### Expectations
- Improves: kasi ya kujifunza
- Does not guarantee: growth kubwa

## How

### Step-by-step method
1. Chagua hypothesis ya growth.
2. Bainisha metric kuu.
3. Weka kipindi cha wiki 1.
4. Endesha jaribio.
5. Andika matokeo na uamuzi.

### Do and don't

**Do**
- Weka hypothesis iliyo wazi
- Tumia metric moja kuu

**Don't**
- Kuendesha majaribio mengi bila mpango
- Kubadilisha metric katikati

### Common mistakes and fixes
- **Mistake:** Hakuna metric. **Fix:** Chagua metric moja ya msingi.
- **Mistake:** Jaribio refu sana. **Fix:** Fupisha hadi wiki 1.

### Done when
- [ ] Majaribio 2 yameandikwa.

- [ ] Matokeo yameandikwa.
